4 — Help Modal

How to Play
Objective

Be the first to play all tiles from your hand. If no one can move, the player with the lowest pip total wins.

On Your Turn
  • Click a tile in your hand to select it
  • Click a highlighted end of the chain to place it
  • If you can't play, click Draw to take from the boneyard
  • If the boneyard is empty, click Pass
Tips
  • Play doubles early — they are the hardest to place later
  • Keep track of the open ends and play tiles that match what you hold
  • Use zoom to see the full chain when it gets long
